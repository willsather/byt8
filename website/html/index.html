features

  • 8 questions

    Eight yes or no styled questions to help you focus on how you felt about today.

  • Daily records

    From midnight to midnight, you have the option to record your reflection anytime.

  • Custom Profile

    Safely secure your information using anonymous cloud storage that only you have access to.

  • Simple Statistics

    Beautiful graphs to show basic insights into your days.

  • Clean Design

    Beautiful graphics make the user experience pleasant and easy to navigate.

  • Smarter Analytics

    As updates are pushed through the App Store, your analytics will eventually be upgraded to support better insights using data you start collecting today.

Weather

Gathers daily weather data to determine how average temperature and forecats affect your mood.

HealthKit

Automatically determines how many steps you took throughout the day and tracks how your overall activity alters your day.

Calendar

Utilizes the day of the week and yearly season to eventually determine what days your good and bad days trend.
